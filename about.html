About Me

Currently working at Smartcity.com in Lagos, Nigeria.

Most of my works revovles around redesigning cities across Africa. My skillset includes: User Research, User Interface Design, Web Development, and Data Analysis.

Just few months ago, I was part of the Stutern Graduate Accelerator 0.2 where I learnt everything about design, research and prototyping. I also had the privilege of building awesome products and worked with fellow cohort on big projects like redesigning the future of transportation in Lagos Metropolis.

I'm yet to launch a start-up, it's not like I'm carrying that in my head. I just want Africa to get better.

I'm currently available for work. If you think your city needs to be like Kigali, Oslo, Amsterdam etc., you can contact me
